# Member Guide - School

Welcome to SpeakUp Connect. This guide covers day-to-day member use for school organizations.

---

## Signing in

Members sign in with:

- **Student ID** (for school-provisioned accounts), or
- **Contact email** (if one is stored on profile),
- plus password.

If your account is pending approval, blocked, or unenrolled, contact school administration.

---

## Home and language

From **Home**, use the **globe** selector, or open **Settings -> Appearance -> Language**.

Help Center loads matching localized markdown where available, otherwise English.

---

## Reports

1. Open **Submit a Concern**
2. Select category
3. Add details and optional photo
4. Submit

Track status in **My Reports**.

---

## Announcements

Use **Home -> Announcements** for school-wide posts.

Members can read, open images, and submit responses when requested.

---

## Alerts

Use **Alerts** for group reminders and response requests.

When an alert is marked **response required**, submit before dismissing.

---

## Settings and profile

Use **Settings** for:

- contact email
- password changes
- language and appearance
- groups and clubs
- Help Center

Personal profile photos depend on organization settings.

---

## Groups and clubs

Use **Browse Groups & Clubs** to request membership.

Use **My Groups & Clubs** to review your rosters and role.

Leaders may have extra roster and alert actions inside their groups.

---

## Support

- Ask school administration for local policy/process questions.
- Use the **Administrator Guide** for admin and staff workflows.
