# Member Guide

Welcome to SpeakUp Connect. This guide covers daily member tasks for school deployments.

---

## Sign in

Use your **student ID** or **contact email**, plus your password.

- **Pending approval**: wait for an administrator to approve your account.
- **Blocked/unenrolled**: contact your school administration for account recovery.

---

## Home and language

From **Home**, use the **globe** language picker, or open **Settings -> Appearance -> Language**.

When a localized help article exists, Help Center loads it automatically. Otherwise, English is shown.

---

## Reports

1. Open **Submit a Concern**
2. Choose a category
3. Add details and optional photo
4. Submit

Track progress in **My Reports**.

---

## Announcements and alerts

- **Announcements**: school-wide posts for all approved members.
- **Alerts**: group-targeted reminders and response requests.

If an alert is marked **response required**, submit your response before dismissing it.

---

## Groups and clubs

Use **Browse Groups & Clubs** to discover and request membership.

Use **My Groups & Clubs** to view your memberships and your role (Leader or Member).

---

## Settings

Settings lets you manage:

- contact email
- password changes
- language and theme
- Help Center access

Personal profile photos depend on your school's organization settings.
