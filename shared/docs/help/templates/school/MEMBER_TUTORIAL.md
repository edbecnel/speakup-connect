# Member Tutorial - School Template

> Audience: First-time students/staff members  
> Purpose: Learn core member flows in order before using `MEMBER_GUIDE.md` as daily reference.

---

## How to use this tutorial

- Complete modules in sequence.
- Perform each **Try this now** task in the app.
- Use `MEMBER_GUIDE.md` when you need feature-by-feature details.

---

## Module 1 - Sign in and account basics

### Goal
Sign in successfully and understand account status states.

### Try this now
1. Sign in with student ID or contact email.
2. Open **Settings** and verify your username/contact email.
3. Identify who to contact for blocked/pending account states.

---

## Module 2 - Home and language

### Goal
Navigate Home and switch app language.

### Try this now
1. Open **Home** and locate quick actions.
2. Change language using the globe selector.
3. Confirm the same setting in **Settings -> Appearance -> Language**.

---

## Module 3 - Reports and status tracking

### Goal
Submit one report and track its status.

### Try this now
1. Open **Submit a Concern**.
2. Complete fields and submit.
3. Open **My Reports** and find your entry.

---

## Module 4 - Alerts and responses

### Goal
Read alerts and submit responses when required.

### Try this now
1. Open **Alerts** and read one entry.
2. If available, submit a response form.
3. Check required-response behavior before dismissal.

---

## Module 5 - Groups and clubs

### Goal
Use group discovery and membership views.

### Try this now
1. Open **Browse Groups & Clubs** and request to join one group (if enabled).
2. Open **My Groups & Clubs** and check your memberships.
3. Identify your role (Leader/Member) if you are in a roster.

---

## Module 6 - Help and support path

### Goal
Know where to get the right type of help.

### Try this now
1. Open **Settings -> Help Center** and read this org's Member Guide.
2. Note who to contact for school policy questions.
3. Note who to contact for platform/app issues.

---

## Completion checklist

- [ ] I can sign in and verify my account details.
- [ ] I can switch language from Home or Settings.
- [ ] I can submit and track a report.
- [ ] I can read/respond to alerts.
- [ ] I can view or request group membership.
- [ ] I know where to get support.
