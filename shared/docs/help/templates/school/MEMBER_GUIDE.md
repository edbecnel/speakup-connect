Welcome to SpeakUp Connect. This guide covers daily use for approved members (students/staff).

---

## Signing in

Members may sign in with:

- **student ID** (when school account provisioning is used), or
- **contact email** (if saved on profile),
- plus password.

### Student ID login

1. Open **Sign In**
2. Enter student ID in **Email or student ID**
3. Enter password
4. Tap **Sign In**

### Email login

1. Enter contact email in **Email or student ID**
2. Enter password
3. Tap **Sign In**

### After admin password reset

Use the new password provided by school staff. Student ID and contact email both map to the same account.

### Account status states

- **Pending approval**: wait for admin approval before feature access.
- **Blocked/unenrolled**: contact school administration for status restoration.

## Home dashboard

Home includes:

- quick actions (reports, announcements, etc.)
- **My Groups & Clubs** (when you belong to at least one group)

### App language

Change language from:

- **Home -> globe selector**, or
- **Settings -> Appearance -> Language**

Help Center loads matching localized markdown when available, else English.

---

## Announcements

**Home -> Announcements** contains organization-wide posts.

Members can:

- read posts
- view pinned posts first
- open post images
- submit responses when requested

Leaders/staff may also compose posts if permitted by role.

---

## Submit a report

1. Open **Submit a Concern**
2. Select category
3. Enter factual details
4. Optional photo attachment
5. Choose anonymous or identified submission
6. Submit

Track status from **My Reports**.

---

## Alerts and reminders

**Alerts** contains group alerts, reminders, and related notifications.

### Responding to alerts

When response is requested:

1. Open alert
2. Complete form (free text, checkbox, or multiple choice)
3. Submit response

If an alert is marked **required**, you must respond before dismissal.

---

## Settings and profile

Use **Settings** to manage:

- contact email
- password changes
- language and appearance
- My Groups & Clubs
- Help Center and app info

### Profile photos

Schools may keep an official photo on file. Personal photo uploads depend on org settings.

---

## Groups and clubs

### Join a group

1. Open **Browse Groups & Clubs**
2. Select a group
3. Request to join (if enabled)
4. Wait for leader/admin decision

### View your groups

See your membership from:

- **Home -> My Groups & Clubs**
- **Settings -> My Groups & Clubs**

Entries typically show leader/member role and optional club position labels.

### Group leaders

If you are a **Leader** on a roster, you may be able to:

- manage roster members
- review join/leave requests
- send group alerts
- edit limited group settings
- post announcements on behalf of group (if enabled)

---

## Privacy and safety

- Share only needed details in reports.
- Use anonymous reporting when personal safety may be affected.
- Follow official school emergency channels for urgent danger.

---

## Getting more help

- Ask your adviser/school office for local policy questions.
