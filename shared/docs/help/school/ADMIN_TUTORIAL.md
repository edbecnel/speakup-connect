# Administrator Tutorial

Audience: First-time school administrators and delegated staff.

Run modules in order. Keep this tutorial mobile-friendly and actionable.

---

## Module 1 - Orientation

1. Open **Settings** and confirm **Administration** visibility.
2. Identify which admin tools your account can access.
3. Open **Administrator Guide** for reference context.

---

## Module 2 - Join and member workflows

1. Open **Join Applications**.
2. Review and process one pending application.
3. Open **Member Management** and verify account status changes.

---

## Module 3 - Groups and clubs

1. Open **Groups & Clubs (Administration)**.
2. Open or create one test group.
3. Review at least one roster or membership request path.

---

## Module 4 - Publishing flows

1. Draft one announcement.
2. Draft one group alert.
3. Verify behavior with approval required ON/OFF.

---

## Module 5 - Roster and grade operations

1. Open **Student Roster** and review one student record.
2. Open **School Grades** and verify grade-level setup.
3. Confirm the impact on member/roster screens.

---

## Module 6 - Translation moderation basics

1. Open **Translations**.
2. Review one string in target language.
3. Save or approve one entry if your role allows it.

---

## Module 7 - Help Center handoff

1. Open **Settings -> Help Center**.
2. Verify **Administrator Guide** and tutorials load.
3. Share this tutorial with new admin staff for onboarding.
