# Administrator Tutorial

Audience: first-time administrators and delegated staff.

This fallback tutorial is used when an organization-type tutorial bundle is unavailable.

---

## Start here

1. Open **Help Center** and read **Administrator Guide** first.
2. Confirm your assigned role and capabilities before running admin workflows.
3. Complete these core flows in order:
   - join applications
   - member management
   - groups and clubs
   - announcements and alerts
   - translation moderation (if enabled)

---

## Troubleshooting quick checks

If an admin menu or action is missing:

1. Verify you are signed in with the correct account.
2. Verify your role has the required capability.
3. Sign out and sign back in after role changes.
4. Confirm the feature is enabled for your organization.

---

## Need school-specific steps?

Use the organization-type tutorial when available (for example, school tutorial bundles) or ask your organization administrator for local operating procedures.
