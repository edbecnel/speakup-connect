# Speakup Connect: Introductory Scope for MONHS

## Purpose

Speakup Connect is a school-focused reporting, communication, and announcement platform intended to give students a safe, structured way to submit concerns, complaints, suggestions, safety reports, and incident reports to authorized school personnel.

For MONHS, the initial goal is to support responsible student voice, improve communication between students and school leadership, and provide a clear process for reviewing reports and sharing important school information.

## Intended Platforms

Speakup Connect will be offered through:

- Android mobile app
- iOS mobile app
- Website access for supported users

## Initial School Scope

The first school version of Speakup Connect is intended to support:

- Student submission of reports, concerns, suggestions, and school-related feedback
- Secure login for students, administrators, and authorized staff
- Role-based access so only approved school personnel can view and manage reports
- Organized report review, status tracking, and follow-up
- School-wide announcements from authorized school personnel
- Group-targeted bulletins for specific grade levels, sections, organizations, or user groups
- School-specific configuration, including MONHS branding and approved user roles
- Basic notifications related to report updates, announcements, and bulletins
- Privacy notice, terms of use, and responsibility disclaimer acceptance during first-time use

## Primary Users

### Students

Students may use Speakup Connect to submit good-faith concerns, safety issues, incidents, complaints, or suggestions. They may also receive school-wide announcements and bulletins targeted to their assigned groups.

### School Administrators and Authorized Staff

Authorized MONHS personnel may review submitted reports, manage report status, publish announcements, send targeted bulletins, and respond according to approved school procedures.

### SSLG Coordinator and Student Leaders

The SSLG coordinator and designated student leadership may help introduce the platform, encourage responsible use, gather feedback, and support adoption among students.

## Responsibilities

Speakup Connect provides the technical platform for secure reporting, communication, announcements, bulletins, access control, and record organization.

MONHS remains responsible for deciding who may access reports, who may publish announcements or bulletins, how reports are reviewed, what school procedures apply, and how safety, discipline, counseling, or child-protection matters are handled.

Speakup Connect is not an emergency response service, counseling service, police service, or replacement for official school procedures.

## Initial Pilot Focus

The initial MONHS pilot should focus on:

- Confirming the school’s desired report categories and workflow
- Identifying authorized administrators and staff users
- Defining who may publish announcements and group-targeted bulletins
- Confirming initial student groups, grade levels, sections, and organizations
- Training school admins on report review, announcements, bulletins, and response procedures
- Preparing student-facing instructions
- Confirming privacy, consent, and parent or guardian requirements
- Testing the app and website with a limited group before wider rollout

## Initial Success Criteria

The first version should be considered successful if MONHS can:

- Safely onboard authorized users
- Allow students to submit reports through the app or website
- Route reports to the correct school personnel
- Publish school-wide announcements
- Send bulletins to selected student groups
- Review, update, and track reports consistently
- Maintain appropriate privacy and access controls
- Gather feedback for improving the next version

## Summary

Speakup Connect is intended to begin as a practical school reporting, communication, and announcement tool for MONHS. Its first purpose is not to replace existing school leadership, guidance, discipline, or child-protection processes, but to support them with a clearer, safer, and more organized way for students to be heard and for important school information to reach the right people.
